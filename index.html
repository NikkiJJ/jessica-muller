ABOUT JESS

With years of expertise as a yacht chef, Jes crafts bespoke menus that cater to every preference and dietary need, ensuring each dish delights both your palate and your eyes.

From the freshest local ingredients to the most intricate plating, Jess's talent for combining flavors with a unique twist with elevate your vacation dining experience. Having hones her skills across luxery yachts since 2015, including completing the prestigious Ballymaloe Cookery School programme in Ireland, she brings a world of culinary mastery straight to your table.

Let Jess make your time onboard a feast for all the senses, with every meal designed to reflect her dedication and culinary artistry.
